About the Luthier

Amherst Guitar Works is dedicated to the meticulous care and restoration of stringed instruments. Whether it's a vintage heirloom or a modern workhorse, every instrument receives uncompromising attention to detail.

Our philosophy is simple: an instrument should feel effortless to play and sound its absolute best. We use a blend of time-honored traditional techniques and modern precision tools to achieve perfect results.
